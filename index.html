Xabier Villanueva Loureiro

GAME DESIGNER · GAME DEVELOPER

I'm Xabier, a Game Designer based in Spain. Currently looking for new exciting job oportunities across the world.

As a game designer, I'm mostly interested on worldbuilding, system, combat, narrative design and storytelling.

In my free time, I'm working on a post-apocalyptic tabletop RPG.

Ronin-Hood Sisters

Ronin-Hood Sisters is a Tactical RPG with elements of Rogue-Lite, where the player takes control of a group of magical girls with the ability to combine elements using their special powers that come from their costumes.

Zero Stealth

Zero Stealth is a stealth, puzzle and humor game. In it, we take the role of Agent Garcia, using his "skills" in stealth, exploration and undercover operations.

Gatopompas

Gatopompas is a 2D platformer game where you play as a sleeping cat having a dream. Try to ascend through the level while avoiding those pointy origami by jumping into bubbles.
